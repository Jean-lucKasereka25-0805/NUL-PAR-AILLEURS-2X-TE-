The Air Jordan brand has redefined the relationship between athletes and their footwear since its inception in 1984.

By blending basketball performance with high-street fashion, Michael Jordan created a global phenomenon that continues to dominate culture.

Each release serves as a milestone in sneaker history, celebrating specific achievements in Jordan's legendary career.

The commitment to premium materials like full-grain leather and Durabuck ensures every pair is built for durability and comfort.

Whether on the court or on the streets of Nairobi, Jordan shoes remain a symbol of flight, ambition, and greatness.
