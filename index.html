Legacy Gallery

Air Jordan is more than just a shoe; it is a cultural movement that began in 1984.

The brand was built on the incredible athletic feats of Michael Jordan during his NBA career.

Designers like Tinker Hatfield revolutionized the industry by adding visible Air units and exotic prints.

Today, Jordan Brand continues to lead the market in both performance basketball and luxury lifestyle.

From the "Banned" 1s to the sleek 11s, every pair carries a piece of sports history.
