Welcome

Michael Jordan is widely regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time. His influence on sports, fashion and culture is unmatched.

Jordan dominated the NBA during the 1990s with the Chicago Bulls, winning six championships.

His competitive spirit and work ethic inspired millions of athletes worldwide.

The Air Jordan brand became one of the most successful sneaker lines ever created.

Today, Jordan remains a global icon and business leader.

About Jordan

Jordan played for the Chicago Bulls and Washington Wizards.

He won 5 MVP awards and 6 Finals MVP awards.

He scored 32,292 career points.

Jordan revolutionized basketball marketing.

He is also the owner of an NBA franchise.
