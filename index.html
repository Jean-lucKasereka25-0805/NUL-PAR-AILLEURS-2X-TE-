AIR JORDAN I

THE ONE THAT STARTED IT ALL

The Legacy

Since his game-winning shot that brought championship glory to North Carolina, Michael Jordan has been at the forefront of basketball consciousness.

He took the court in 1985 wearing the original Air Jordan I, simultaneously breaking league rules and his opponents' will while capturing the imagination of fans worldwide.

The "Banned" colorway became a symbol of rebellion and style, cementing the sneaker as a cultural icon beyond the hardwood of the NBA.
